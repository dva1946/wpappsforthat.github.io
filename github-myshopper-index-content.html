MyShopper Introduction

Concept of MyShopper comes from using a popular Grocery Shopping List for three years. It has a 4.9 rating from 70,000 reviews. It is loaded with features, bells and whistles, yet missing (or hidden within its settings) are two very critical features to be rated excellent: Unique item names and store location, nothing else matters if you can't quickly get it. Thus the birth of MyShopper for WordPress by WpAppsForThat.Com.
