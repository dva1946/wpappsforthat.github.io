We have to admit living with 23,000 other retired people gets old, thus we welcomed the 10 day trips to and from Alaska. I'd traveled to and purchased considerable un-developed properties in Sterling and Donna hadn't been there since we re-connected in 2011 - 2013 timeframe.

The Alaska adventures start for me (Dave) in 1996 - 1998 with two jobs up there. The 2nd one took me to Anchorage, Alaska as a project manager on a telecom billing system conversion for 5 months (June - October). While there in 1998 fishing took control of my off-times, and eventually wondered down to the Kenai Peninsula to fish Salmon on the Kenai River. Donna and I lived in Colorado for most of our adult-lives.

In 2013, nearing retirement, we purchased a retirement home in Green Valley, Arizona (some of my family lived there at the time). Prior to retirement we lived separate lives until meeting in 1986, but we already had other lives, so time our lives together had to wait. Finally in 2013 we again got together, and this time for good.
